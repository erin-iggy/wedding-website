Our Story

We lived a few doors down from one another during our first year at Brandeis and bonded over walking to class every morning, studying together, and gaming on Friday nights. Even when Iggy had to return to Singapore for military training, we stayed in touch through occasional messages every now and then.

Several years later, fate brought us back together as we kept bumping into each other on campus. We picked up from where we left off and rekindled our friendship. From poster symposiums to steak dinners, movies dates, and late-night strolls by Boston Harbor, we fell in love, and the rest was history.
